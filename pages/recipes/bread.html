A very boring and generic bread.

Ingredients:

  • water
  • yeast
  • salt
  • Some boring flour

Directions:

  1. Start oven at 200 degrees celcius
  2. Mix together wather with the yeast untill it's a homogenuous mixture.
  3. Add salt until it's to salty to eat.
  4. Add flour until the dough is so dry it's not sticking to your fingers anymore.
  5. Put some plastic wrap on top and let it rest for 2 hours.
  6. Bake it in the oven for 10 minutes and serve imediatly.
