The best pizza dough recipe

Ingredients:

  • water
  • yeast
  • honey
  • Tipo 00 flour

Directions:

  1. Mix together wather with the yeast untill it's a homogenuous mixture.
  2. Add honey to activate the yeast and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  3. Add flour until the dough is so dry it's not sticking to your fingers anymore.
  4. Put some plastic wrap on top and let it rest for 2 hours.
  5. If you followed the directions correct, the dought should have doubled in size. Now put it in the fridge for 48 hours.
  6. That's it, start making pizza!
